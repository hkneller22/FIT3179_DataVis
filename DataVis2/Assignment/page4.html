How is the workforce being affected?

- In the left unit-chart, brightness/hue encodes the age group, whilst the shape encodes the relevance of their highest level of study to their current job. In addition, the number of a particular shape encodes the number of people with the same relevancy. Each shape represents 100k people. From this figure, it can be observed that relatively fewer people are working in a field unrelated to their field of study.
